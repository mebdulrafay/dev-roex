[ 01 ]  ABOUT

Engineering at every layer of the stack

I'm a dedicated developer who treats every build like a circuit: each part has a job, and the whole only works when the connections are precise. That mindset carries from soldering a sensor onto a breadboard to shipping a production-ready interface — I care about what happens underneath as much as what the user sees.

Based in Jauharabad, Pakistan, I split my time between hardware prototyping and software craft, using AI-assisted workflows to move faster without cutting corners. Whether it's an ESP32 reading the world in real time or a web app reading a user's intent, the goal is the same: turn a rough idea into something that runs reliably, looks deliberate, and holds up under load.
